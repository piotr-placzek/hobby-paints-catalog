About

Hobby Paints Catalog (HPC)

This is a simple application to finding paints and their replacements for painters of figures and models.
App is totally free and open source.
Feel free to contribute, and DON'T USE IT for commercial purposes.

Making this app takes me few hours of coding ;)

If you are enjoying with this app and you find it useful, you can always buy me a coffee.

Sources

Product inventory such us catalog numbers, names and other information are obtained from paint manufacturers' online stores or catalogs.

The list of replacements comes from various open sources, a list of which can be found below.

  • Mini Emporium Samiego
  • The Army Painter Colour Conversion Chart

You can also find other conversion sources on the internet.
I'm working on ensuring that my app also includes conversions from the sources below.
